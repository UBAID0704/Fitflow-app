# FitFlow рҹҸғвҖҚвҷӮпёҸ
### Fitness App for Busy Office Professionals

> *Designed during UX/UI Design Internship at **Decodelabs***

---

## рҹ“Ң Project Overview

**FitFlow** is a mobile fitness application designed for busy office professionals who struggle to find time for exercise. The app delivers short, structured workouts that fit into a packed workday вҖ” no gym, no commute, no excuses.

---

## рҹҺҜ Problem Statement

Office professionals want to stay fit but face a consistent set of barriers:

- No time between back-to-back meetings
- Guilt-heavy fitness apps that punish missed days
- Generic plans that ignore desk-job posture pain
- Exhaustion by evening that kills motivation

**FitFlow solves this by putting the user's real schedule first.**

## рҹ—әпёҸ Empathy Map

| | Details |
|--|---------|
| рҹ‘Җ **See** | Fit colleagues on social media В· Long gym queues В· Healthy meal ads В· Office chairs dominating their day |
| рҹ’ӯ **Think** | "I'll start properly on Monday" В· "I don't have time today" В· "Other people manage it вҖ” why can't I?" |
| вқӨпёҸ **Feel** | Guilty about inactivity В· Overwhelmed by complex plans В· Anxious about long-term health В· Hopeful when starting a new app |
| рҹҸғ **Do** | Downloads apps, rarely opens after day 3 В· Sets early alarms, hits snooze В· Googles quick workouts but never starts |

---

## рҹ’Ў Key Research Insights

| Insight | Design Implication |
|---|---|
| Time is the #1 barrier | Show workout duration upfront вҖ” offer 5, 10, 20-min options |
| Guilt kills motivation | Replace streak penalties with flexible, encouraging reminders |
| Beginners feel excluded | Use plain language, prominent beginner filters |
| Desk pain is real | Include a dedicated Desk Stretch category |
| Social validation matters | Add optional progress sharing or accountability feature |
